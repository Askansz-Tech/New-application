Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 3, 2025

PixelHeaven values your privacy. We do not collect or share personal information. All settings and data are stored locally on your device unless you enable optional features such as anonymous analytics or error reporting.

  • Local Storage: Your preferences and data are saved only on your device.
  • Cookies: Used for session management, not for tracking or advertising.
  • Analytics (optional): If enabled, only anonymous usage data is sent to help us improve the app. No personal data is collected.
  • Error Reporting (optional): If enabled, crash and bug reports are sent anonymously to help us fix issues faster.
  • Privacy Demo: Shows privacy information on your dashboard for transparency.

Your Choices: You control all privacy-related features in the settings. You can enable or disable analytics, error reporting, cookies, and local storage at any time.

Contact: For questions or concerns, please contact us via our GitHub page.
